Uprizer Labs LLC

Overview

Uprizer Labs LLC is consulting and product development company founded by brothers Ian and Andrew Clarke in 2008.

Services

We've been working with machine learning since Ian built his first neural network at the age of 16 in 1993. Since then Ian and Andrew have applied machine learning to solve real-world problems in areas as diverse as ad targeting, people matching in online dating, oil and gas, finance, and fraud detection.

Products

Commercial

  • 33Mail - An extremely useful tool to protect you from unwanted email

Open Source

  • QuickML - A powerful and fast random forests implementation (with significant contributions by OneSpot).
  • LastCalc - A programmable online calculator
  • Swarm Framework - Prototype for a distributed computation system that goes far beyond "MapReduce"

Note regarding "Uprizer Inc"

While they share a name and Ian Clarke's involvement, Uprizer Labs LLC is legally unrelated to Uprizer Inc, a company founded by Ian Clarke, Rob Kramer, and Steven Starr in 2000. Uprizer Inc became inactive around 2004. Any information regarding "Uprizer" prior to 2008 likely pertains to Uprizer Inc, not Uprizer Labs LLC.
